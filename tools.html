A running list of software, utilities, and apps I rely on day to day. Nothing fancy — just stuff that earned a permanent spot on my machine.

OpenSteamTool

An open-source alternative to SteamTools based on custom .DLL files for Steam. It grants access to games and DLCs using .lua files and decryption keys, featuring a built-in manifest downloader for seamless content management.
